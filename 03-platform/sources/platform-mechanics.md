# Platform Mechanics Documentation

## Complete Features Guide

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2020). "Your Complete Guide to Poshmark's Features & Tools." Poshmark Blog. May 7, 2020.

**URL:** https://blog.poshmark.com/2020/05/07/your-complete-guide-to-poshmarks-features-tools/

**Key Content:**

### Bundles
- Buyers can add multiple items from same seller to a bundle
- Sellers can offer bundle discounts
- Single shipping charge for bundled items
- Private bundle comments between buyer and seller

### Posh Parties
- 4 daily themed virtual shopping events
- Categories rotate (brands, styles, departments)
- Host picks featured listings
- Increased visibility for shared items during parties

### Offer to Likers
- Sellers can send private offers to users who liked their items
- 24-hour expiration window
- Minimum 10% discount required
- Discounted shipping option available

### Posh Stories
- Short-form video/photo content
- 24-hour visibility window
- Shopping integration (link to listings)
- Engagement features (likes, comments)

### Additional Features
- Closet sharing: Manual and automated options
- Following system: Feed of followed closets
- Comments: Public listing discussions
- Love Notes: Post-purchase reviews

## Shipping Documentation

### Current Shipping Policy (2025)

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2025). "Lower shipping starting September 12th." Poshmark Blog. September 8, 2025.

**URL:** https://blog.poshmark.com/2025/09/08/lower-shipping-starting-september-12th/

**Key Content:**
- $6.49 flat rate shipping (reduced from previous $7.67)
- USPS Ground Advantage service (2-5 business days delivery)
- 5 lb weight limit per package
- Prepaid, pre-addressed labels provided
- Buyer pays shipping (included in purchase total)
- Free shipping promotions available to sellers
- Tracking included on all shipments

### Posh Post System
- Sellers print shipping label from app/website
- Labels pre-paid and pre-addressed
- Drop-off at USPS or schedule pickup
- 7-day shipping window after sale
- Tracking updates in-app for buyer and seller

## Fee Structure

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (n.d.). "Get Started on Poshmark Today!"

**URL:** https://poshmark.com/posh_guide/get_started_on_poshmark

**Key Content:**

### Commission Structure
- Sales ≥$15: 20% commission to Poshmark
- Sales <$15: $2.95 flat fee to Poshmark
- Seller receives remaining amount as earnings

### Shipping Costs
- Buyer pays $6.49 flat rate shipping
- Seller can offer discounted or free shipping (seller absorbs cost)
- Overweight packages: Seller charged difference

### Payment Processing
- No additional payment processing fees
- Direct deposit to linked bank account
- Minimum payout threshold: $0 (any balance can be withdrawn)

### Example Calculations
- $50 sale: Seller receives $40 (80% of $50)
- $10 sale: Seller receives $7.05 ($10 - $2.95)
- $100 sale with free shipping: Seller receives $73.51 ($100 × 0.80 - $6.49)
