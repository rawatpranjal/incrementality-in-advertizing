# SEC Filings and Regulatory Documents

## SEC Filings (2020-2022)

### Form S-1 (IPO Prospectus) - December 2020

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2020). Form S-1 Registration Statement. Filed December 18, 2020.

**URL:** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1825480/000119312520320132/d66583ds1.htm

**Key Facts:**
- Commission structure: 20% for sales ≥$15, $2.95 flat fee for sales <$15
- 20.5 billion social interactions recorded in 2019
- Asset-light business model with no inventory ownership
- Founded 2011, headquarters in Redwood City, California
- Primary categories: Women's, Men's, Kids, Home, Pets, Electronics

### Form 10-K (Annual Report 2020) - March 2021

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2021). Form 10-K Annual Report for fiscal year ended December 31, 2020. Filed March 11, 2021.

**URL:** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1825480/000156459021014879/posh-10k_20201231.htm

**Key Facts:**
- 558 employees as of December 31, 2020
- Posh Post: Prepaid, pre-addressed shipping labels via USPS
- Posh Authenticate: Authentication service for items ≥$500
- Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) and Active Buyer metrics disclosed
- Risk factors including competition, platform dependence, regulatory environment

### Form 8-K (Naver Acquisition Announcement) - October 2022

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2022). Form 8-K Current Report. Filed October 3, 2022.

**URL:** https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1825480/000119312522257206/d252462dex991.htm

**Key Facts:**
- $1.2 billion acquisition by Naver Corporation (South Korean technology company)
- $17.90 per share cash consideration
- Strategic rationale: Naver's technology capabilities and Asian market access
- Deal structure: All-cash merger agreement

## Post-Acquisition Context (2023-Present)

### Acquisition Completion

**Citation:** Poshmark, Inc. (2023). "Naver Completes Acquisition of Poshmark." PR Newswire. January 6, 2023.

**URL:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/naver-completes-acquisition-of-poshmark-301714299.html

**Key Facts:**
- Poshmark became wholly-owned subsidiary of Naver Corp on January 6, 2023
- No individual SEC filings required after acquisition (private company)
